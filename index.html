Rui Zhang

I am a first-year PhD student supervised by professor Andrew Luo at Musketeers Foundation Institute of Data Science, The University of Hong Kong. I received my Master's degree in Computer Science from the School of Computer Science, Fudan University. Before that, I was a so-called "accountant" with a Bachelor's degree from the School of Management, Jilin University. My research interests include 3D reconstruction, 3D perception, and video generation.
